About Me

I have two main goals as a photographer.

1) to always make my clients and models feel beautiful. I feel that everyone has unique, and beautiful aspects about them, and I love helping others realize their beauty and potential through my work.

2) to constantly be learning. One of the things that I love the most about photography is the fact that I don't know everything, and I probably never will. I am constantly learning and exploring, and that excites me to no end.
